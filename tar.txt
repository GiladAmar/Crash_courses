Compression:
    tar -cvf
        -cvf compress, verbose, file/archive
        -z to-> tar.gz
        -j -> bz2

    e.g.    tar -cvf output.tar folder_contents/ another_whole_folder

    In terms of archive size:
        tar > gz > bz2

Extraction:
    -xvf extract
    -t list content
    e.g. tar -xvf input.tar

    # To specify which file to extract
        tar -xvf it.tar it.doc  #or *.doc

    # To add to the archive
        tar -r it.tar add_this_file.doc
